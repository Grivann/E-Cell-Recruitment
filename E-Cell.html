VISION

The Entrepreneurship Cell of IIT-Roorkee, previously known as Entrepreneurship Development Cell IIT Roorkee (EDC IITR), has been established with the objective of creating, fostering and promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship among the youth of the nation. Created in the early months of 2006, the Entrepreneurship Development Cell has already taken rapid strides in cultivating entrepreneurial temper and has succeeded in carving a niche for itself.

CULTURE

E-Cell has been one of the epitome groups in the campus in terms of its culture. We, the E-Cell perpetrate every initiative with utmost zeal and acumen, the assorted group catalogues as everyone is oriented towards one goal of taking entrepreneurship to the populace. We are quite ardent about experimenting with new ideas, to take exploit the unexplored so we take up new substantial and improved initiatives whenever possible. Our precept is to support and grow so as to have fun and learn, for the same we have intra-group hangout sessions, Founder Interactions, Team discussions, webinars and Annual trips.
